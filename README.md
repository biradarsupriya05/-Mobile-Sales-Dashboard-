# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

### 📌 Project Overview

- The Mobile Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across multiple dimensions such as brands, mobile models, cities, payment 

- methods, and time periods. The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, track Month-to-Date (MTD) performance, compare sales with the Same Period Last Year (SPLY), and gain

- actionable insights for better decision-making.
  
### 📌 Project Highlights

- 📅 MTD (Month-to-Date): Measures cumulative sales from the beginning of the current month up to the selected date, helping track ongoing monthly performance.
  
- 📈 Same Period Last Year (SPLY): Compares current sales with the corresponding period from the previous year to evaluate Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and identify seasonal trends.
  
- 📊 KPIs: Monitors key business metrics, including Total Sales, Total Quantity Sold, Total Transactions, and Average Selling Price (ASP).

### 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 💰 Overall Sales Performance

-	Total Sales reached 769M with 19K units sold across 4K transactions.
  
-	The Average Selling Price (ASP) is 40.11K, indicating a strong mix of mid- to premium-priced smartphones.
  
### 📊 City-wise Sales Insights

-	🏆 Delhi generated the highest sales with 204M, followed by Mumbai with 127M.
  
-	📈 Chennai and Ranchi were the next best-performing cities, each contributing around 31M in sales.
  
-	📊 Most other cities recorded sales between 23M–27M, indicating a balanced sales distribution across regions.
  
-	📉 Kanpur, Kolkata, and Vadodara recorded the lowest sales at 23M, highlighting opportunities for targeted marketing and sales growth.
  
### 📈 Quantity by Month
-	July recorded the highest sales quantity (1,700 units), indicating peak customer demand. 

-	February had the lowest sales (1,451 units), suggesting a seasonal slowdown. 

-	Sales recovered steadily after September, showing positive momentum in the final quarter. 

### ⭐ Customer Ratings

-	Good ratings (2,331) account for the majority of customer feedback.
  
-	Poor ratings (852) are relatively low, indicating strong customer satisfaction. 

-	Maintaining product quality and after-sales service can further improve customer experience. 

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis

-	UPI is the most preferred payment method (26.36%). 

-	Debit Card (24.72%), Credit Card (24.69%), and Cash (24.22%) contribute almost equally. 

-	The balanced payment distribution suggests customers prefer multiple payment options. 

### 📱 Top Mobile Models

-	iPhone SE generated the highest sales (60M). 

-	OnePlus Nord (58M) and Galaxy Note 20 (56M) are also among the top-performing models. 

-	Premium and upper mid-range smartphones dominate customer demand. 

### 🏷️ Brand Performance

-	Apple leads with 161.6M in sales and 783 transactions. 

-	Samsung follows closely with 160.0M, while OnePlus ranks third with 153.7M. 

-	These three brands contribute the largest share of total revenue. 

### 📅 Sales by Day of Week

-	Monday and Friday recorded the highest sales (26.4M each). 

-	Thursday had the lowest sales (23.2M). 

- Running promotions during lower-performing weekdays could help improve overall weekly sales.

  ### 💡 Business Recommendations
  
- 📈 Increase inventory for top-selling brands and mobile models to avoid stock shortages.

- 🎯 Strengthen marketing campaigns during low-performing months and quarters to improve sales consistency.

- 🌍 Focus on high-potential cities while implementing targeted promotions in underperforming regions.

- 💳 Promote digital payment methods (UPI, Debit/Credit Cards) through cashback and discount offers.

- ⭐ Improve customer experience by addressing poor ratings and enhancing after-sales service.

- 📊 Monitor MTD and Same Period Last Year (SPLY) trends regularly to identify growth opportunities and respond quickly to sales declines.

- 🛍️ Introduce seasonal offers and festive promotions during weaker sales periods to drive demand.

- 📦 Use demand forecasting to optimize inventory planning and reduce overstock or stockouts.

-  🤝 Leverage high-performing brands for cross-selling and bundled offers to increase average transaction value.

- 📅 Review quarterly performance to refine sales strategies and achieve sustainable year-over-year growth.
